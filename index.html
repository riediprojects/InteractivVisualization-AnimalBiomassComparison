"The number of farm animals has been increasing worldwide for decades. Humans are taking more and more advantage of animals to use them for their own purposes.
Be it cows to get milk from them, chickens to get their eggs or be it to process them into meat to eat them. These farm animals are almost exclusively kept in factory farms. Most people are not aware of the incredible suffering we do to these animals every day. And even less are most people unaware of the extent of this suffering.
This page wants to illustrate this problem to the visitors, so that people start to think about this misery."
